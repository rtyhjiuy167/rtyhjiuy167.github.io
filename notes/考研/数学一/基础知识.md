#### 泰勒中值定理

$$
\begin{aligned}
&设f(x)在x=x_0的邻域内 n+1 阶可导，则f(x)=f(x_0)+f'(x_0)(x-x_0)+⋯+\frac{f^{(n)}(x)}{n!}(x-x_0)^n+\frac{f^{(n+1)}(ζ)}{(n+1)!}(x-x_0)^{(n+1)}
\end{aligned}
$$
